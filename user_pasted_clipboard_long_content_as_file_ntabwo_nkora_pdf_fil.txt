## 2. HYBRID MODEL EXPLAINED

### 🔹 OPTION A: PRE-ORDER SYSTEM (Cash Flow Engine)
**Process:**
1. Client atanga deposit 30-50% (3M-5M RWF)
2. Ugura imodoka hanze (Be Forward, SBT Japan, Car Junction)
3. Shipping → Dar es Salaam → Kigali (25-40 days)
4. Client yishyura balance iyo imodoka igeze
5. Delivery & after-service

**Math Example:**
- Car cost (CIF): $3,000
- Shipping+Clearing: $2,000
- Total cost: $5,000 (~6.5M RWF)
- Selling price: 8M RWF
- **Profit: 1.5M RWF** (23% margin)

**Advantages:**
- Nta risk ya stock imara igihe
- Nta storage costs
- Cash flow ihoraho

### 🔹 OPTION B: RESELLING (Profit Maximizer)
**Process:**
1. Gura imodoka 1-3 ziri stock
2. Parking/display
3. Quick sales (immediate delivery)
4. Higher margins

**Math Example:**
- Buy: 8M RWF
- Sell: 11M RWF  
- **Profit: 3M RWF** (37% margin)

### 🔹 HYBRID STRATEGY (BEST)
**Month 1-2:** Pre-order only (build trust, no stock risk)  
**Month 3:** Fata profit y'pre-order → gura imodoka 1 stock  
**Month 4+:** Combine both (stock for quick sales + pre-orders for cash flow)

**Suppliers:**
- Be Forward (beforward.jp) - Best for beginners
- SBT Japan (sbtjapan.com) - Variety nyinshi

**Logistics:**
1. Purchase online
2. Payment (Wire transfer/Telegraphic Transfer)
3. Shipping to Dar es Salaam (20-30 days)
4. Clearing agent (Dar)
5. Transport Kigali (truck/bus)
6. Rwanda Revenue Authority taxes

- CIF Price: $3,000-8,000

- Transport Dar-Kigali: $400-600
