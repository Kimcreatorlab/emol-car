# Product Requirements Document (PRD): EMOL CAR CO LTD Digital Ecosystem

## 1. Executive Summary
**Project Name:** EMOL CAR CO LTD Digital Transformation
**Industry:** Automotive Dealership & Brokerage (Rwanda)
**Core Value Proposition:** "30% Deposit Model" - making quality car ownership accessible through transparent importing and flexible financing.
**Primary Language:** Kinyarwanda
**Secondary Language:** English

---

## 2. Strategic Objectives
*   **Market Dominance:** Position EMOL CAR CO LTD as the #1 digital car dealership in Rwanda by 2026.
*   **Conversion Optimization:** Transform social media "noise" (TikTok/IG) into qualified WhatsApp leads and closed sales.
*   **Trust Building:** Utilize 3D branding, leadership transparency, and documented processes to eliminate common "import scams" fears.
*   **Omnichannel Presence:** Seamlessly integrate Mobile PWA, Desktop Web App, and Social Channels (WhatsApp, TikTok, IG, FB, LinkedIn).

---

## 3. Targeted User Personas
1.  **The Corporate Professional:** Looking for reliability and comfort (RAV4, GLE). Needs flexible payment plans.
2.  **The Business Owner:** Needs workhorses (Hilux) or status symbols (Land Cruiser). Often pays full amount for Magerwa stock.
3.  **The Hustler/Driver:** Seeking fuel-efficient entry-level cars (Vitz, Corolla) for ride-hailing/taxi business.

---

## 4. Functional Requirements

### 4.1 Frontend Experience (Web App & PWA)
*   **30% Deposit Calculator:** Real-time calculation of upfront commitment vs. total landed cost.
*   **Localized Interface:** Kinyarwanda-first UI with English toggle.
*   **Discount Engine:** Clear visualization of "Price Before" vs. "Price After" with percentage badges.
*   **Magerwa Port Stock:** Dedicated inventory section for immediate-purchase vehicles located at the port.
*   **Global Sourcing Indicators:** Visual flags for South Korea, China, Dubai, and Belgium imports.

### 4.2 Lead Management & Conversion
*   **WhatsApp Integration:** Direct "Chat on WhatsApp" triggers for every vehicle listing.
*   **Community Links:** Persistent access to the official WhatsApp Group and Channel.
*   **Social Grid:** Unified follow buttons for all 6 strategic channels.

### 4.3 Leadership & Authority
*   **Team Profiles:** Dedicated section for CEO, Manager, and Marketing Manager to humanize the brand.
*   **Trust Triggers:** RDB Certification badges, 147-Point Inspection guarantees, and Bank Partnership logos (BK).

---

## 5. Visual Identity & Brand Guidelines
*   **Logo:** 3D Metallic Chrome Branding (Automotive Premium).
*   **Palette:** Deep Blue (#1E3A8A), Vibrant Orange (#F97316), and Metallic Silver/White.
*   **Typography:** Montserrat (Headlines) and Open Sans (Body) for a balance of modern authority and readability.
*   **Imagery:** High-fidelity car photography with "Action/Lifestyle" context.

---

## 6. Technical Specifications
*   **Platform:** Full-stack Web App with PWA (Progressive Web App) capabilities.
*   **Navigation:**
    *   *Desktop:* Sidebar-driven dashboard for high-density information.
    *   *Mobile:* Bottom navigation bar for thumb-friendly app-like experience.
*   **Performance:** Optimized for low-bandwidth mobile environments in Rwanda.

---

## 7. Roadmap & Future Phases
*   **Phase 1 (Complete):** Core Home Screens, 30% Calculator, Social Integration.
*   **Phase 2 (Next):** Marketing Manager Operations Dashboard, Refer & Earn referral system.
*   **Phase 3:** Real-time Import Tracking for customers (Japan -> Dar -> Kigali).
*   **Phase 4:** Regional Expansion UI for Uganda and Tanzania markets.
