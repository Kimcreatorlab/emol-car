The 6-Step Sales Funnel

Here is how someone goes from not knowing you to paying $15,000:

1. Awareness TikTok video reaches 10,000 people (100%)
2. Interest 500 click to WhatsApp link (5%)
3. Consideration 100 qualified leads (budget confirmed) (20%)
4. Intent 30 request invoices/proposals (30%)
5. Purchase 10 pay 30% deposit (33%)

Key Insight: From 10,000 viewers, we convert 10 to paying customers (0.1% conversion rate). This is industry standard for high-ticket items.
